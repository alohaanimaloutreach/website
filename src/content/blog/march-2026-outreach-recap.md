This past Saturday, our volunteer team headed out to the Waiʻanae Coast for our monthly outreach visit. Rain or shine, we show up — and this month, the sun was on our side.

## By the Numbers

- **47 dogs** received wellness checks
- **120 lbs** of dog food distributed
- **32 dogs** treated with flea and tick prevention
- **4 dogs** flagged for follow-up veterinary care

## What We Saw

The community along Farrington Highway continues to grow, and so does the need. Many of the dogs we see each month are well-loved pets whose owners simply can't afford veterinary care. That's where we come in.

One highlight from this visit: a pitbull mix named Koa, who we've been monitoring for a skin condition since January. After two months of medicated baths provided by our team, his coat is finally growing back. His owner, James, told us, "You folks are the only ones who've ever helped us out here."
